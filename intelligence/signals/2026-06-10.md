## Notable

- **[Anthropic / MCP]** The **MCP 2026-07-28 spec Release Candidate** is published (post dated 21 May 2026; RC locked, final ships 28 Jul 2026). Hardens authorization via six SEPs aligned to OAuth 2.0 / OIDC: formalizes MCP servers as OAuth **Resource Servers**, mandates **Resource Indicators (RFC 8707)**, adds `iss` validation (RFC 9207), DCR `application_type`, issuer-bound credentials, refresh-token + step-up scope clarifications. Directly relevant to ThunderID MCP work (#2973/#2974/#2975). Slightly older than this run but logged for baseline. — [MCP blog](https://blog.modelcontextprotocol.io/posts/2026-07-28-release-candidate/) — capability: C2, C4, C7, C10 — domain: IAM-for-AI

- **[IETF OAuth WG]** Active cluster of AI-agent auth drafts in circulation: `draft-oauth-ai-agents-on-behalf-of-user` (now at -02; `requested_actor`/`actor_token` delegation), `draft-rosenberg-oauth-aauth` (AAuth, OAuth 2.1 agentic ext, -01), `draft-oauth-transaction-tokens-for-agents-04` (actor/principal context), `draft-klrc-aiagent-auth-01` (WIMSE+SPIFFE+OAuth). *Inference:* revision numbers suggest recent updates, but exact revision dates could not be confirmed (datatracker unreachable — see notes). — [on-behalf-of draft](https://www.ietf.org/archive/id/draft-oauth-ai-agents-on-behalf-of-user-01.html) · [AAuth draft](https://www.ietf.org/archive/id/draft-rosenberg-oauth-aauth-00.html) · [txn-tokens-for-agents](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/draft-oauth-transaction-tokens-for-agents-04) — capability: C3, C7, C10 — domain: IAM-for-AI
