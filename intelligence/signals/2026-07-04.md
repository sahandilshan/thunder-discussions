## High signal

- **[Nuggets]** **`langchain-nuggets` 1.0.0** — a shipping, open-source **action-time authority enforcement + verifiable-delegation** layer for the two dominant agent frameworks (LangChain / LangGraph), released **July 3, 2026** and available now on **PyPI** (`pip install langchain-nuggets`) and **npm** (`@nuggetslife/langchain-nuggets`). The on-taxonomy hook: it **intercepts every tool call before it executes, checks five constraints per call against a signed delegation credential, and either allows the action + emits a cryptographic proof or blocks the call entirely** (runtime least-privilege agent authorization + delegated authority — **C3**). Each allowed action produces a signed **Action Receipt** recording *who acted, on whose authority, under which constraints, with what declared intent*; receipts reference hashes of params/results (not raw data), are **signed RS256 against Nuggets' published public key, and verify independently** by a partner, regulator, or a system on a different cloud without access to the originating org's logs (capability-scoped, independently-verifiable proof — **C7**-adjacent). Logged High: net-new to our logs, genuinely in-window (July 3), corroborated across two independent outlets, and a **concrete shipping artifact** (v1.0.0 on PyPI/npm) rather than a vision/funding item — it maps directly onto ThunderID's delegated-authorization-for-agents / grants-as-flows surface and the verifiable-delegation theme (cf. the AIP draft, OAuth transaction-tokens-for-agents), warranting a deep-dive on how "signed delegation credential + per-action receipt" compares to ThunderID's flow-scoped grants. — [National Law Review — Nuggets Brings Verifiable Authority Enforcement to LangChain and LangGraph](https://natlawreview.com/press-releases/nuggets-brings-verifiable-authority-enforcement-langchain-and-langgraph) / [Biometric Update — Nuggets, Innovation Labs advance governance for autonomous AI agents](https://www.biometricupdate.com/202607/nuggets-innovation-labs-advance-governance-for-autonomous-ai-agents) — capability: C3, C7 — domain: IAM-for-AI
