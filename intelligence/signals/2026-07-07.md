- **[IETF / draft-mcguinness-oauth-actor-proofs]** Net-new to our logs and the **freshest item this sweep**: **`draft-mcguinness-oauth-actor-proofs-00`** — *OAuth Actor-Signed Hop Proofs* (v00, dated **July 4, 2026**), an **optional companion profile** to `draft-mcguinness-oauth-actor-profile-00` (logged 07-05). It introduces an **`actor_proofs` claim: a signed per-hop proof chain** in which the actor added at each visible delegation hop signs its own participation and the target binding it authorized for that hop — i.e., cryptographic, per-hop non-repudiation layered on top of the RFC 8693 `act`-claim delegation model. Directly on ThunderID's delegated-agent-authorization / token-exchange surface (**C3** agent authorization, **C7** token security for AI, **C10** standards participation), and part of the verifiable-delegation-chain theme (cf. DAAP hash-chained audit 07-05, SPICE actor-chain below, Nuggets 07-04, AIP). **Logged Notable, not High:** net-new to logs but an **individual (non-WG-adopted) submission dated July 4** — near-window (3 days old) but not a verified July 6–7 WG movement. — [datatracker — draft-mcguinness-oauth-actor-proofs](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-mcguinness-oauth-actor-proofs/) — capability: C3, C7, C10 — domain: IAM-for-AI

- **[IETF / SPICE actor-chain + intent-chain cluster]** Net-new to our logs by name, folding into the broad IETF agent-identity individual-draft cluster prior runs track (all **out-of-window**, individual/non-WG): **`draft-mw-spice-actor-chain-05`** — *Cryptographically Verifiable Actor Chains for OAuth 2.0 Token Exchange* (dated **April 25, 2026**; Prasad/Oracle, Krishnan/JPMorgan Chase, Lopez/Telefónica, Addepalli/Aryaka) defining **six delegation-chain disclosure profiles** (Declared/Verified × Full/Subset/Actor-Only) that preserve and validate delegation-path continuity across successive token exchanges — RFC 8693 permits nested `act` but leaves prior actors informational-only, which this closes; and its sibling **`draft-mw-spice-intent-chain-00`** — *Cryptographically Verifiable Intent Chain for AI Agent Content Provenance*.
